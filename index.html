ABOUT ME

Being Passionate for technology and having a desire to learn and explore more makes Siddharth what he is. He is a Community Intern at Progate, Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador, AngelHack Student Ambassador, Google Crowdsource Influencer, Hackathon Mentor and Lead Organizer for Hack This Fall Hackathon.

He like to do new things, discover the unknown and spread knowledge. He leaves no stone unturned to complete his tasks.

He can Transform the unknown Possiblity to Achievability, Enthusiastic of creating Solutions, Imagination at its peak. Like to Learn to Lead, taking Community together to Sail.

His passion for Hackathon is immense and he has been part of 50+ Hackathons with different roles. He is a community guy and a public speaker.

Additionally travelling, music and food is what he likes the most.
